

Making image ready for slider background as shown in demo.


1) Download and install pattern for photoshop from http://subtlepatterns.com/graphy/

2) Open slider background.psd provided with this download.

3) Double click on smart object.

4) insert your image. (prefer dark one or you can use photoshop tools to make it dark)

5) Close and save the document.

6) You will see pattern and gaussian blur applied which you adjust to suit your requirements.

6) GO to File > Save for web & devices optimize and save.

6) You are done.
